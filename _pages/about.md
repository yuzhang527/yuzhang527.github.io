I am a junior undergraduate student at Peking University, majoring in Statistics. I worked as a research intern at PKU Interpretable Large Language model And Reasoning (PILLAR) Group, advised by Prof. Liangming Pan.

My research interests broadly lie in machine learning and artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on mechanistic interpretability. I am guided by the belief that meaningful research should deepen our understanding of the internal mechanisms of machine learning models while enabling reliable and trustworthy behavior in real-world applications.
